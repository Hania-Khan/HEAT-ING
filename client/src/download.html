Do you qualify for a new boiler?

The answer to this is yes if:

  • You are a homeowner or renting

  • You are receiving benefits

  • Your boiler is more than 10-years old

Benefits include:

  • Income Related ESA

  • Pension Credit Guarantee

  • Income Based JSA

  • Universal Credit

  • Child Benefit

  • Pension Savings Credit

  • Tax Credits
